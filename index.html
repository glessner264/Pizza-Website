Welcome to Wild Flames Pizza and Wings website.






Wild Flames Pizza & Wings is a family run business located in Oakdale PA. At Wild Flames we use the old style stone fired pizza method to make the pizza the best that we can. We provide deliveries within a 20 mile radius of our store. If you live near Oakdale Pennsylvania this website is a simple way for you to order pizza & wings online and get your order delivered in a timely manner. Click on “Order Now” to start your order, then click the next button at the bottom of the page or click the section you want in the navigation to continue your order.

About Us

In 1980 a man named Wade wanted to start his own business, so he decided to start a family run pizza shop. Over the years Wild Flames has gotten more and more publicity in the Pittsburgh area and is currently one of the locals favorite places to get pizza. Wild Flames is the last pizza shop in the Pittsburgh area to use a stone fired oven to make there pizzas. Customers say that the stone fired oven brings out the best taste possible in the pizza. We hope that over the years Wild Flames can become an even bigger business so we can build more pizza and wings shops in the Pittsburgh area.
