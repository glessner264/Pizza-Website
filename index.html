About Wild Flames Pizzza & Wings

Welcome everyone to Wild Flames Pizza & Wings. We have one pizza shop and it is located in Oakdale Pennsylvania. Our goal here at Wild Flames is to provide a simple way for you to order pizza & wings online and get your order delivered to you in a timely manner. Click on “Order Now” to start your order, then click the next button at the bottom of the page or click the section you want in the navigation to continue your order. History

Wild Flames Pizza & Wings is a family run business located in Oakdale PA. Here at Wild Flames we use the old style stone fired pizza to make the pizza the best that we can. Currently there is only one Wild Flames pizza shop, in the future we are planning on opening more here in the area of Pittsburgh. As a business we proved deliveries to all are customers within a 20 mile radius of our store. In 1980 a man named Wade Glessner wanted to start his own business, so he decided to start a family run pizza shop. Over the years Wild Flames has gotten more and more publicity in the Pittsburgh area and is currently one of the local’s favorite places to get pizza. Wild Flames is currently the last pizza shop in the Pittsburgh area to use a stone fired oven to make their pizzas. Customers say that the stone fired oven brings out the best taste possible in the pizza. We hope that over the years Wild Flames can become an even bigger business so we can build more pizza and wings shops in the Pittsburgh area.
